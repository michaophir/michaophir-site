Start with Profile & Skills — upload your resume, extract your skills, generate your scout config, scout for open roles, and review your ranked matches in Discover Roles.

## The 3-step flow

**1. Upload your resume**
Go to Profile & Skills and upload your PDF. RoleScout parses your resume and automatically generates target companies and role filters tailored to your background.

**2. Scout for open roles**
Go to Scout and hit Start Scouting. Scout checks your target companies job boards and returns ranked matches in 1-2 minutes.

**3. Review your matches**
Go to Discover Roles. Filter by remote/hybrid/onsite, search by keyword, save roles you like, pass on the rest.
